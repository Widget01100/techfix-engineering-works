Who We Are

Founded by Moffat Shikuku, TECHFIX ENGINEERING WORKS is a Nairobi-based mechanical and fabrication company committed to delivering high-quality, reliable, and cost-effective engineering solutions. Our expertise spans latheworks, welding, crane repair, and fabrication, serving both industrial and individual clients across Kenya.

At Techfix, we believe in combining technical precision with innovative problem-solving. Every project we take on — from machinery repairs to custom metal fabrication — is handled with professionalism and a focus on long-term performance.
