About TechFix Engineering Works

Precision Engineering | Performance You Can Trust

Who We Are

Founded by Moffat Shikuku, TechFix Engineering Works is a Nairobi-based engineering firm providing precision machining, metal fabrication, welding, and technical maintenance solutions. We are committed to quality workmanship, innovation, and customer satisfaction across Kenya and East Africa.

Our Mission

To deliver engineering solutions that combine accuracy, durability, and affordability while building long-term client trust through excellence and integrity.

Our Values

  • Integrity – Honesty and transparency in all operations.
  • Quality – Delivering precision and performance every time.
  • Innovation – Continuous improvement and creative engineering solutions.
  • Customer Focus – Building strong partnerships through service excellence.

Our Team

Our team of skilled technicians and engineers brings over a decade of experience in mechanical design, fabrication, and industrial maintenance. From crane repairs to custom machining, we turn technical challenges into lasting solutions.
