About TechFix

Founded by Moffat Shikuku, TechFix Engineering Works is Nairobi's premier production engineering workshop. With over 25 years of hands-on experience, we deliver precision machining, welding, fabrication, and specialized repair services.

Our team handles everything from simple lathe works to complex CNC machining, industrial crane repairs, and custom gym equipment fabrication.
